Acorn

GL Extraction & Validation for QuickBooks Online

1
Connect a QBO Company

Click + Add at the top of the sidebar to authorize Acorn with your QuickBooks Online company.

2
Select a Client

Once connected, click the company name in the sidebar. The report configuration panel will appear.

3
Set Your Date Range

Choose a start and end date. Acorn will pull all GL transactions and month-end balances for the selected period.

4
Generate Report

Click Generate Report. Acorn will fetch your GL data, build 8 structured Excel tabs, and prepare the file for download.

5
Download

When complete, click the download link to save your Excel file.

6
What You Get

IS GL Detail · BS GL Detail · BS Balances · P&L · Balance Sheet · IS by Class · IS by Location · Validation — with live cross-check formulas proving GL completeness.
